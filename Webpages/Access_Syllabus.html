How to Access the Syllabus

Watch this informational video about how to access the syllabus.

Also, below are some step-by-step guides if the video wasn't enough!

Step 1:

The student will first start at the D2L main page. They will then select the course that they would like to view the syllabus for from their pinned courses.

Step 2:

The student can then access the syllabus through the content browser section on the course’s main page or by using the “Course Tools” drop-down menu, selecting “Content”, and clicking the tab labeled “Syllabus and Schedule”.
